Pairing Process Guide

From stranger to trusted adult in 4 days.

The pairing process is the foundation of every successful ABA session. This guide walks you through it, day by day.

D1
Observe & Shadow

Place ZERO demands on the child

Introduce yourself warmly, observe how the child spends their time, and shadow the parents. Your only job today is to be present and safe.

D2
Connect to Reinforcers

Introduce yourself as someone fun

Follow the child's lead entirely. Connect yourself to their known reinforcers — if they love swings, push them. If they love the park, go together.

D3
Increase Structure

Begin controlling access to reinforcers

Spend more time in the work area, increase demands-to-breaks ratio, and begin incorporating social reinforcers into play.

D4
Grab Bag Day

Introduce novel reinforcers & child follows your lead

Arrive with your Grab Bag of novel items. Restrict access until demands are met. Begin having the child follow your lead — the relationship is built.
